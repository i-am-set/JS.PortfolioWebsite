Privacy & Consent

What This Site Collects

Page view counts only. No names, IP addresses, or personal data are stored. The view counter increments once per page load when analytics consent is granted. No data is ever shared with third parties.

How Consent Works

Consent is stored in a first-party cookie named portfolio_consent. It expires after 180 days and uses SameSite=Strict.

Cookie Categories

Necessary: Always active. Required for the site to function. No personal data.
Analytics: Optional. Controls the page view counter. Enabled only with your consent.
